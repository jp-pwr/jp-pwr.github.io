Mystical power of Japanese language

18 Oct 2022

Here’s an upcoming presentation on Japanese language/culture:


Mystical power of Japanese language 
(Mistyczna moc języka japońskiego)


date: 26.10.2022 (Wednesday)
time: 18:45-20:15
language: English
place: room 112 (H-4)


In this presentation, I will talk about the history of Japanese language, starting from its origins, until the formation of the language that we use today. I will also explain what “language” means to Japanese people, and how their belief affects its everyday usage.

This is an open presentation. Feel free to invite your friends.

Information page: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ne3JSSOBepy-1k7L6XhKWkMUiEyMmD_97--5fpkLzvc/edit?usp=sharing
